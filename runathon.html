Receive the light and pass it on.

Client

Wellington College Super Six

Project

Logo Development and Logo Animation

Runathon exudes the spirit of Wellington College. It's about giving back. It brings people together; regardless of age or interests or towards a goal for the greater good. To me, it's a charity event that perfectly embodies the school motto, "Receive the light and pass it on." That's why the logo depicts a baton of light being passed on.

 The reveal

Revealing the Runathon logo is always an exciting moment. This year, for lack of a more professional word, I wanted it to feel epic. Get people excited about the event! Make it feel big and real.
